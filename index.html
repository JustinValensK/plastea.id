Problem

Plastic waste has always been a major problem in environmental pollution, both land and sea pollution. The nature of plastic waste is that it does not decompose easily, the processing process causes poison and is carcinogenic, it takes hundreds of years to decompose naturally. For marine pollution, Indonesia is the second largest producer of marine plastic waste in the world. Therefore, we make places or containers to collect plastic waste and make it into handicrafts rather than leaving it alone.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is becoming a pioneer in creating refreshing and healthy drinks, while protecting and restoring Indonesia's environment which is threatened by plastic waste. We are committed to reducing our plastic footprint, promoting environmental awareness, and building a cleaner, more sustainable future for future generations. Our mission is building public awareness through our campaign and inviting people to care by helping support waste recycling by buying our products or exchanging their waste for definite benefits, namely drinks.
